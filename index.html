.about us

.at NAD.café we love three things: tasty coffee, healthy food, and good sound (nada in sanskrit) .come to our rooftop bar - the only of this kind in Lumbini - and enjoy good music, a cool breeze during hot days and the sunset .we are part of the NADA YOG Sound Healing and Meditation Centre inviting you to enjoy a cup of coffee and good food after having immersed in sound healing, yoga and meditation.

.we proudly serve organic coffee from Nepal that is roasted by a locally owned and operated roasters .every single step of the brewing process is artisanal to create the best taste .every saturday you can learn how to brew your favorite coffee yourself .but tea and juice lovers are not forgotten and can choose among diverse sorts of organic teas from the Himalayan mountains and fresh juices.

.we believe in the power of healthy food prepared with seasonal, regional and organic ingredients .we work together with local farmers of the nearby communities helping them to practice organic farming and avoiding long transport ways .we serve vegan and vegetarian food, mostly sugar-free and with gluten-free options .our menu is small but always enlarged by additional dishes and new creations depending on the available fruits and veggies of each season.

.we believe in the power of sustainable behaviour by supporting zero waste and plastic-free consumption .be part of the solution, not part of the pollutionand bring your own reusable containers and cups if you wish for take away .you can also lend our containers/ cups against a deposit or buy them
