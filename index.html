.about us

At NADCAFE we love three things: tasty coffee, healthy food, and good sound (nad in Sanskrit). Come to our rooftop bar - the only one of this kind in Lumbini - and enjoy a cool breeze and the sunset. We are part of the NAD Sound Healing Centre inviting you to enjoy a cup of coffee and good food after having immersed in sound healing, yoga and meditation.

We proudly serve organic coffee that is created by a locally owned and operated roaster. Every single step of the brewing process is artisanal to create the best taste. Of course, you can also chose among diverse sorts of organic teas from the Himalayan mountains and fresh juices.

We believe in the power of healthy food prepared with seasonal, regional and organic ingredients. We work together with local farmers of the nearby communities helping them to practice organic farming and avoiding long transport ways. We serve vegan and vegetarian food with sugar and gluten free options. Our menu is small and varies depending on the available fruits and veggies of each season.

We believe in the power of sustainable behaviour by supporting zero waste and plastic-free consumption. So, be part of the solution, not part of the pollution and bring your own reusable containers and cups if you wish for take away. You can also lend our containers/ cups against a deposit or buy them.
